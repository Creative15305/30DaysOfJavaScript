Checkers Rules

  • Red starts first. Players alternate turns.
  • Pieces move diagonally forward. Kings move both ways.
  • Capture by jumping over opponent’s piece.
  • Multiple captures allowed and required if available.
  • Reach the last row to become a King.
  • Captures are mandatory when available.
  • Win by capturing all opponent pieces or blocking all moves.
